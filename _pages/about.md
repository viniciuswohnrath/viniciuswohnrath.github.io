I am a sociologist of education affiliated with the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). My research focuses on political sociology, historical sociology, religion and politics, and the sociology of education in Latin America.

My current work examines the intersections between education, political transformation, religion, and state formation in Latin America, with particular attention to Brazil and Mexico.

## Research Interests

- Sociology of Education
- Historical Sociology
- Political Sociology
- Religion and Politics
- Latin American Studies
